Where: LaGuardia Co-op

Demo Days is a monthly student-run event in NYC, organized by Tech@NYU, Parsons Code Club, Create@Cooper, and ColumbiaADI.
We're all about fostering a community of students who create things. We want to provide a platform for student builders to present their work, to celebrate their creations, and to let that inspire other students to build projects they care about.
